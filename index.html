NJ Institute of Science and Technology was established in the year 1991 and is now imparting quality education with the most modern teaching techniques available to enable its students to compete with the world. It provides a congenial atmosphere for the total development of the whole personality and its integration with the society.

It is committed to academic excellence, and appreciation of the worth of the individual student and educational leadership. The school tries to inculcate in students the values of personal hygiene, neatness in uniform. cleanliness of surroundings, graceful manners. obedience and discipline.

The children are provided with an environment. which fosters a spirit of enquiry and keen competition Personal attention is given to individual child. A competent and supporting staff takes care of the special needs of the children with full commitment.

Learning is a joy here and the school provides a very vibrant and inspiring atmosphere for the students to grow in a stress-free environment.
